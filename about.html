About Me

I'm an interaction designer based at MIT. I focus on creating innovative home products that grows around users to make lives more interesting.

I grew up in Taiwan, dabbled in many fields including physic, mechanical engineering, and computer science. I always ended up making projects that have an human interaction component to it, and decided that I should focus on creating intelligent objects for daily use.

I enjoy bringing ideas to life through sketching, making, and coding. I believe we can all use techonolgy that are defined by the everyday users so the interactions can be personalized, and make the day-to-day creative and diverse.

In my free time, you'll find me playing guitar, breakdancing, or painting. Feel free to email me with any inquiries!
