Polaroid Drawings

MIT 4.022 2023, Feb 2023

Class Project

Design/Industrial Design

This project consists of multiple drawings, to scale, of the same polaroid camera model. Achieving an accurate image without losing the overall form by focusing only on detail was an important part of making this project. On the accuracy side, extracting information from exsisting pictures is the crucial element of making accurate drawings. Measurements and important guidelines remain in the drawing as a way to hint towards the initial design of the camera, and important proportions can be seen. On the representation side, lineweights, layers, notations, and different line densities are experimented. Different line types are used to shown heirachy and used to drawn focus of the viewers towards certain details.
