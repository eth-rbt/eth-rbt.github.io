Microscale Fastening System in
Mechanical Watches

MIT Culpepper Lab , Oct 2022- Sept 2023

Research Project

ProductDesign/Mechanism

In this long-term project, we aim to develop a new type of microscale fastening system in mechanical watches. This is constraint by the size, material, and the highly-precise manner in mechanical watch designs. We aim to increase the shock absorb ability and reduce its rate of failure to ensure watch quality. Furthermore, the ease of manufacturing and assembling is put into consideration. We are working to develop a patent for our design. Due to it's nature, some of the proprietary pictures and results aren't shown on this page.
