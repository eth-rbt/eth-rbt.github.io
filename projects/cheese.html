Panoroma Cyanotype Camera

Design/Photography

Say Cheese! This is a camera that takes a panoramic photo of the sky and the surrondings and projects it onto a flat surface using a cyanotype process. The camera is designed to rethink the process of a digital picture, where it is noramlly fast and single direction oriented. This wooden camera requires the user to adjust the focus of each lens, load a cyanotype paper, and leave it under the sun for around 3-4 hours, making the process thought provoking. The wooden finish draws inspiration from cheese with texture and how cheese blocks normally has multiple holes.

Collaborator: Quincy Kuang
